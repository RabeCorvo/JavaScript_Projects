You are walking along and see some lovely apples at the market.


You are carrying 4 apples and see a cute monkey.


You chase the monkey and corner it in an alleyway.


You grab a stick and prepare to brawl with the monkey troop.


You pick yourself back up as the monkeys all laugh at you. Enraged you dive at the closest monkey and continue the brawl.


Huffing and puffing you make your way through the sprawled out bodies of the monkeys to collect your apples.


You stand there with your apples and notice the original cute monkey getting to its feet. Maybe you should give it one of your apples afterall?


You feel stronger from this crazy encounter.
